## How to use this

- **Cadence:** run the set ~monthly (AI answers drift over time).
- **Track:** prompt × engine × result. Suggested columns: `Prompt | ChatGPT | Perplexity | Gemini | Copilot | Google AIO | Notes`. Mark each as: ❌ not mentioned · ✅ mentioned · 🔗 cited with link.
- **Win conditions:**
  - Sections 1–4 → you want to be **named** in the answer.
  - Section 5 → you want your page **cited as a source**.
  - Section 6 → you want the info to be **accurate** (matches the site + schema).

## What the results tell you

- **Missing from #1–#2 (discovery/local):** off-site authority gap → prioritise **Google Business Profile** (both locations), **IndiaMART / ExportersIndia / TradeIndia / Justdial**, **LinkedIn**, and a few quality backlinks.
- **Missing from #3–#4 (product/industry):** strengthen product/industry page content + internal links; ensure each product's FAQ answers buyer intent.
- **Not cited in #5 (informational):** add comparison/answer articles (e.g. "Glassine vs Greaseproof", "Choosing cupstock GSM") with stats and citations.
- **Wrong/empty in #6 (entity):** entity gap → add real `sameAs` social URLs + confirmed founding year to the schema, set up **Google Business Profile** and a **Wikidata** entry.
